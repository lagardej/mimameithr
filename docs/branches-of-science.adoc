= Branches of science
:toc: left

Reference taxonomy. Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Branches_of_science

== Formal sciences

A priori, no empirical grounding.

* Logic
* Mathematics (pure/applied)
* Statistics
* Theoretical computer science
* Information theory
* Systems theory
* Decision theory

== Natural sciences

Empirical study of the physical universe.

=== Physical science

* Physics
* Chemistry
* Astronomy (→ Space science)
* Earth science (→ Geology, Oceanography, Meteorology, Hydrology, Atmospheric science)

=== Life science (Biology)

* Biochemistry
* Microbiology
* Botany
* Zoology
* Ecology

== Social sciences

Human behaviour, society, culture.

* Anthropology
* Archaeology
* Economics
* Education
* History
* Human geography
* Law
* Linguistics
* Political science
* Psychology
* Sociology

== Applied sciences

Practical application of the above.

* Engineering
* Medicine
* Pharmacy
* Agricultural science
* Computer science
* Accounting
* Business administration
* Urban planning
