= Kvasir

Kvasir was a being of supreme wisdom, created from the combined knowledge of the gods.
He was slain and his blood mixed with honey to produce the mead of poetry — the source of all wisdom and knowledge.
Kvasir is the science knowledge layer for Mimameithr.

All simulations in Kvasir are *stateless pure functions*.
They take parameters and return results.
They have no dependency on ECS, time, or orchestration.
