= Kvasir

Knowledge library for Mimameithr.
Provides domain-neutral scientific knowledge used across the simulation — mathematics, physics, geodesy, and eventually social sciences.

Kvasir follows the standard classification of science into three major branches.

== Formal sciences

The study of formal systems using a priori methodology.
Concerned with abstract structures defined by rules and definitions, independent of empirical observation.

[cols="1,3"]
|===
|Namespace |Content

|`Kvasir.Formal.Maths`
|Geometry, spatial partitioning, interpolation, hashing, random
|===

== Natural sciences

The study of natural phenomena — cosmological, geological, physical, chemical, and biological.

[cols="1,3"]
|===
|Namespace |Content

|`Kvasir.Natural.Physical.Astronomy`
|Orbital mechanics, stellar geometry
|`Kvasir.Natural.Physical.Physics`
|Radiative transfer, solar geometry
|`Kvasir.Natural.Physical.Geodesy`
|Planetary coordinate systems, discrete global grids (`IGeodesicGrid`, `LatLng`)
|===

== Social sciences

The study of human behaviour and the functioning of societies.
Covers anthropology, economics, history, and related disciplines needed for the Anthroposphere simulation.

Currently empty — populated as the Anthroposphere develops.
